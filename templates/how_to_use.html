Quick Start

Use the system in 4 simple steps

This page explains how to set up your camera, choose an exercise, and use the tracker correctly so the counter and movement feedback work as expected.

1

Open the workout page

Start from the home screen and press Start Your Exercise to enter the live tracking page.

2

Position yourself clearly

Stand far enough from the camera so the important joints for the selected exercise are visible in the frame.

3

Select an exercise

Use the exercise buttons on the right panel. The guide image and short instructions will update automatically.

4

Follow the motion guide

Perform the movement while watching the live camera feedback, rep counter, stage label, and reference image.

Before You Start

Checklist

  • Make sure your camera is allowed and working.
  • Use a bright area so your body landmarks are easier to detect.
  • Wear clothing that makes your arm and leg movement visible.
  • Keep enough space around you for safe movement.

During Exercise

Best Practice

  • Keep your movement controlled instead of rushing the repetitions.
  • Try to match the general shape shown in the exercise guide image.
  • If the counter is inaccurate, step back slightly and re-center your body.
  • Use the Reset Counter button when starting a new set.
